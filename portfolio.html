About Me

Coder

Currently I am working as a Technical Project Manager at Judlau Contracting. Prior to employment with Judlau, I owned and operated a small tech company specializing in 3d modeling for the automation heavy machinery. In the evenings, I attend school at Northwestern University to pursue a career in computer programing. Outside of work and school, I volunteer with Team Rubicon responding to natural disasters around the United States. I also sit on the Illinois Tollway 3d Design Standards Board of review to help integrate 3d models into the construction process. My favorite hobbies are fishing, hiking and building websites. A Weird fact about me... I was born with 11 fingers.
